My Education

Shenzhen University

My favorite class in Shenzhen University was Program of Innovation-type Projects, I learned MATLAB programming language in this class.
So I was able to joined 2017 American College Student Mathematical Modeling Competition, held by ICM, and I won the third prize.

A memorable memory from Shenzhen University was one time I participated in a school debate and won the title of the best debater

Seattle University

My favorite class in Seattle University was ESL. I made so many friends from different countries.

My memorable memory from Seattle University was the night I went out with my classmates to eat Pho on the end of the course.

Stevens Institute of Technology

My favorite class in Stevens Institute of Technology is Web Programming, thought it is challenge.

My memorable memory from this school is that I spent many nights in the library catching up on homework until it closed at 12:00.
